**Usage**: "Never abandon your dreams." - Always hold on to your aspirations, no matter the challenges you face.

**Synonyms**: leave, desert, forsake, relinquish
**Antonyms**: keep, retain, support, continue

**Antonyms**: noble, dignified, hopeful, proud
