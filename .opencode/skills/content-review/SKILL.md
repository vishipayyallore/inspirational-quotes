## What I do

- Scan vocabulary files for correct `## Word` → `**Meaning**:` → `**Usage**:` structure
- Check that words are in alphabetical order
- Verify quote attribution formatting (`>` blocks + `_— Author_`)
- Flag missing attributions, broken formatting, or duplicate entries

## When to use

Use before creating a PR or committing significant content changes. Focuses on structural compliance rather than educational quality.
