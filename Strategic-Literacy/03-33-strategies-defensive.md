Defensive Insight: Identify hidden resistance early; undefined opposition is more dangerous than open conflict. In technical settings, address passive resistance before it becomes structural.

Defensive Insight: Emotional composure during crisis establishes silent authority. Calm analysis under pressure builds trust.

Defensive Insight: Artificial urgency may be used to bypass scrutiny. Slow down high-risk decisions when pressure feels manufactured.

Defensive Insight: Consensus pressure suppresses necessary dissent. Use objective reasoning to counter momentum toward flawed decisions.
